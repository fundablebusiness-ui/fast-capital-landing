Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about fast business funding

How fast can I really get approved? ▼
Most applications are approved within 24–48 hours. Once approved, funds typically land within 1 business day.
What do I need to provide? ▼
Three months of bank statements, 3+ deposits, and consistent $3K–$15K+ monthly cash flow. That's all we need to get you approved fast.
What happens after I apply? ▼
1) Submit application online. 2) Reviewed by multiple funders within minutes. 3) You'll get approval decision + funding amount. 4) If approved, we confirm with bank statements and you receive a call from our team. 5) Money deposits within 1 business day. Total time: usually under 48 hours.
How much can I borrow? ▼
Up to $2M depending on your business revenue and cash flow. Approval amount is based on your ability to repay.
Do you require collateral? ▼
No collateral required. Funding is based solely on your business's cash flow and revenue. You're not risking your home, equipment, or personal assets.
Do you check my credit score? ▼
We review your business cash flow, revenue trends, and ability to repay—not your personal credit history. Loan options may vary. This makes us ideal for owners with imperfect credit.
What's the cost? Any hidden fees? ▼
Transparent pricing. Funding costs are factored into your repayment term—no surprise documentation fees, application fees, or prepayment penalties. We'll show you the exact total before you accept.
Is this a loan or something else? ▼
This is alternative business financing. It works like a loan (you repay over time) but with faster approval, no collateral, and focus on cash flow instead of credit scores. Terms are flexible based on your business type.
