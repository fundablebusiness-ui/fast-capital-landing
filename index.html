Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How fast can I really get approved? ▼
Most applications are approved within 24–48 hours. Once approved, funds typically land within 1 business day of confirmation.
What do I need to provide? ▼
Three months of bank statements, 3+ deposits, and consistent $3K–$15K+ monthly cash flow. That's all we need to get you approved fast.
Do you check my credit score? ▼
We review your business cash flow, revenue trends, and ability to repay—not your personal credit history. This makes us ideal for owners with imperfect credit.
Do you require collateral? ▼
No collateral required. Funding is based solely on your business's cash flow and revenue. You're not risking your home or personal assets.
How much can I borrow? ▼
Up to $2M depending on your business revenue and cash flow. Approval amount is based entirely on your ability to repay.
What's the cost? Any hidden fees? ▼
Transparent pricing. Funding costs are factored into your repayment term—no surprise fees, application charges, or prepayment penalties. We'll show you the exact total before you accept.
What happens after I apply? ▼
1) Submit application online. 2) Reviewed by multiple lenders within minutes. 3) You'll get approval decision. 4) If approved, confirm with bank statements. 5) Money deposits within 1 business day. Total: usually under 48 hours.
Is this a loan? ▼
This is alternative business financing. It works like a loan (you repay over time) but with faster approval, no collateral, and focus on cash flow instead of credit scores. Terms are flexible based on your business type.
